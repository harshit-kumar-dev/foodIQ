## 📝 Project Brief
**NutriScan (formerly FoodIQ)** is a cutting-edge web application designed to empower users with deep insights into their food choices. By scanning labels and analyzing ingredients against personal health profiles, it acts as a digital nutritionist in your pocket.

## 🔥 Key Features (Final Version)

*   **🔍 Real-Time Scanning:** Instantly extract ingredient lists from any food product using advanced OCR.
*   **🛡️ Health Guard:** Automatically flags harmful ingredients based on your conditions (Diabetes, BP, Allergies).
*   **📊 Nutri-Score:** Visual health grades (A to E) with detailed nutritional breakdowns.
*   **💡 Smart Alternatives:** AI-powered suggestions for healthier market swaps.
*   **📉 Health Tracker:** Interactive dashboard to monitor daily sugar and nutrient intake.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture Overview

Built with a high-performance **React SPA** frontend and a robust **Node.js/Express** backend, leveraging **Gemini AI** for intelligent food analysis and personalized health recommendations.
